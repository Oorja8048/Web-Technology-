GVK EMRI(Emergency Management and Research Institute)is a pioneer in Emergency Management Services in India.As a not-for -profit professional organisation operating in Public Private Partnership(PPP)mode.GVK EMRI is the only professional emergency service provider in India today.GVK EMRI handles medical, police, emergencies through the "1-0-8" Emergency service. This is free service delivered through state-of-art-emergency call response centres and has over 2600 ambulances across Andhra
Pradesh, Gujarat, Uttarakhand, Goa, Chennai, Rajasthan, Karnataka, Assam, Meghalaya and Madhya Pradesh. With the expansion of fleet and services set to spread across more states ,GVI EMRI will have more than 10000 ambulances covering over a billion population by 2011.
